1. Pour milk in a suitable container at room temperature, beat in eggs, add salt and sugar.

2. Gradually add flour, while stirring, so that it does not turn out lumps. They will still be obtained, so stirring must be done in a quality manner. Bring to the consistency of low-fat sour cream. Add baking powder. 
3. Stir everything, leave for 15-20 minutes and then add vegetable oil. By the way, you can leave the pancake dough in the refrigerator and make pancakes later. Nothing will happen to him.

4. Pour a little oil into a very hot pan and fry the pancakes.
